Classes Offered

Headstart offers classes for both students with no programming experience and students with a programming background.

For students with no previous experience programming, we recommend taking Introduction to Python. For students with previous experience, like APCS, we recommend taking Full Stack Development

Located in NoVA

Our classes will take place in the Chantilly area. Exact address will be announced soon.

Schedule and Timing

Our classes will run for 6 weeks on Sundays, from March 3rd - April 7th. Specific course timing can be found on class webpages.

Classes run for 2 hours, but require commitment during the week to work on homework and the cumulative final project. Headstart is looking for motivated students who want to set themselves up for success.

FAQs

  • How is Headstart different from other programming classes?

    Headstart is designed to teach students in a lab-based manner so they learn by doing. Our structured curriculum will help establish foundations and progress every week. We will teach a 90 minute session and follow it up with homework assignments to solidify concepts.

  • What are HeadStart's main goals and values?

    Headstart revolves around its students, everything we do comes from putting their interests first. We are teaching high-quality courses that will give students a "headstart" in their CS careers. Headstart aims to create an open learning environment so our students can feel comfortable engaging in class.

  • How do I know which class to enroll in?

    We are currently offering two classes: Introduction to Python and Full Stack Development. The Python course is designed for beginners and doesn't expect any sort of programming experience. The Full Stack course is meant for students who are more advanced and have taken programming classes (AP Computer Science is a plus). Feel free to contact us if you have any questions about which class to register.

  • How will HeadStart help me after graduating from the course?

    Headstart's community of instructors will help polish resumes and provide advice on getting internships in high school in college. They will also provide college advice and best practices to have a solid CS career.
